Group Name: Practice Project Group 1
Project Title: TaskSlinger

TaskSlinger is an intelligent chatbot that takes voice and text input to help users create tasks in their
Google Calendar. 
This can benefit a wide range of individuals, including Busy professionals, individuals with disabilities, elderly individuals, multi-taskers, students
by automating the mundane act of physically creating, listing, deleting tasks from their online calendars with just a voice or text message.
